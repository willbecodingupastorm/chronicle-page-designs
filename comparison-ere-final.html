Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What does Chronicle monitor in the ERE?

Chronicle monitors the SSA's ARS system daily for documents, status changes, hearing info, and deadline-triggering events.

Do I still need to log into the ERE myself?

For most daily operations, no. You may still log in for specific actions Chronicle doesn't cover.

How quickly does Chronicle detect new documents?

Daily checks. You'll typically see changes within 24 hours of SSA posting.

Is my ERE data secure?

Yes. Chronicle is SOC 2 certified with encryption at rest and in transit.

What CMS systems does Chronicle integrate with?

Clio, Filevine, MyCase, Zapier, and full API access.
