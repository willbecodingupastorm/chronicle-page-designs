Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Chronicle a case management system?

No. Chronicle is an ERE monitoring platform. It monitors SSA activity and routes that information to your existing workflow, but it doesn't replace your CMS. Your CMS manages tasks and firm execution; Chronicle ensures those systems stay aligned with what the SSA is actually doing.

What CMS platforms does Chronicle work with?

Chronicle integrates with Clio, Filevine, MyCase, and offers Zapier connectivity for other platforms. It also provides a full API for firms with custom integration needs. Chronicle is CMS-agnostic and works with any CMS that has an API, or no CMS at all.

How is Chronicle different from Assure/Atlasware?

Both help SSD firms work with the ERE. The difference is workflow stage coverage. Assure is primarily optimized for hearing prep; Chronicle supports the full SSD lifecycle (initial, reconsideration, hearing, and post-hearing) with daily monitoring throughout.

Is Chronicle SOC 2 certified?

Yes. Chronicle is SOC 2 certified and uses encryption for data at rest and in transit. Your firm's data is audit-ready at all times.
